Harrisonburg is a lively small town filled with exciting places within the community. It is the home of James Madison University where college students work towards their passion while interacting with the downtown community. Harrisonburg resides between the Blue Ridge Mountains, making it a fun place to go hiking or camping. If you're in the area, be sure to visit all the local attractions!
